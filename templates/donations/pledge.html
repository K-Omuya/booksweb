How Pledges Work

  1. Fill out the pledge form with the type of donation you'd like to make in the future.
  2. Specify when you expect to fulfill your pledge.
  3. We'll send you a gentle reminder when the date approaches.
  4. Fulfill your pledge when you're ready by donating books or money.
  5. Your contribution will help us plan and allocate resources for future needs.

Why Make a Pledge?

  • Help us forecast and plan for future book donations
  • Allow schools to know what resources they can expect
  • Commit to making a difference on your own timeline
  • Join our community of supporters
  • Make a promise now that will impact students later

Our Impact

Thanks to generous donors and pledgers like you, we've been able to:

  • Distribute over 5,000 books to students in need
  • Support 30+ schools with educational materials
  • Establish book clubs in communities across Kenya
  • Help thousands of students improve their reading skills

Your pledge today helps us continue this important work tomorrow.
