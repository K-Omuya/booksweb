Upload a Book

Share your books with others in the community. For a small fee of 20 shillings, you can facilitate the exchange of knowledge.

How It Works

  1. Fill out the form with details about your book.
  2. Add a photo of the book for better visibility.
  3. Choose whether it's for exchange or donation.
  4. Provide your location and contact information.
  5. Submit the form and wait for interested readers.

Book Exchange Benefits

  • Share knowledge and stories with others
  • Give your books a second life
  • Help students access educational materials
  • Build a community of readers and learners
  • Reduce waste by reusing books

Exchange Guidelines

  • Books should be in readable condition with no missing pages
  • Clearly state if there are any marks or highlights in the book
  • Be responsive to messages from interested readers
  • Handle meet-ups or drop-offs responsibly
  • Consider donating books that are in high demand for education
