Our Story

Books for All was founded in 2020 by Derrick Onsarigo, who witnessed firsthand the challenges many students face in accessing educational materials. Inspired by his own experiences and the needs he saw in communities across Kenya, Derrick envisioned a platform that would connect those who have books with those who need them.

What started as a small community initiative has grown into a nationwide movement, with book clubs established in schools across Kenya and thousands of books exchanged and donated to students in need.

2020

Books for All was founded with the first book exchange program launched in Nairobi.

2021

Expanded to 5 counties and established the first 10 school book clubs.

2022

Reached 1,000+ book donations and launched the digital platform for wider accessibility.

2023

Partnered with national organizations to expand book clubs to 20+ counties.

2024

Celebrating our impact on over 5,000 students and planning expanded services nationwide.
