About Us

Books for All is dedicated to bridging educational inequality by ensuring every student has access to books through donations and exchanges, fostering a culture of learning and knowledge sharing.

Our Mission

To bridge the educational inequality by ensuring every student has access to books through donations and exchanges, fostering a culture of learning and knowledge sharing.

Our Vision

A future where no child lacks books and education is accessible to all.
