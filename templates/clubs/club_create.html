Create a Book Club

Start a book club at your school to promote reading and serve as a collection point for book donations.


Benefits of Book Clubs

  • Access to Books

    Receive book donations and serve as a distribution point for educational materials.

  • Promote Reading Culture

    Encourage students to develop a love for reading and lifelong learning.

  • Build Community

    Create a space for students to discuss books and share ideas.

  • Recognition

    Become part of a national network of school book clubs recognized for promoting literacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many students do we need to start a book club?
There is no minimum requirement, but we recommend at least 10 students to create an engaging environment for discussions and activities.
Are there any costs involved?
No, registering a book club is completely free. Books for All provides resources and support at no cost to schools.
How soon will we receive books after registering?
After your club is approved, you can expect to receive an initial donation within 2-4 weeks, depending on your location.
Can primary and secondary schools both participate?
Yes, we welcome book clubs from both primary and secondary schools across Kenya.

Requirements

  • A dedicated teacher or staff member to serve as the club patron
  • A secure space to store and manage books
  • Regular meetings (at least twice a month)
  • Commitment to maintaining records of book distributions
  • Participation in occasional Books for All events and initiatives
